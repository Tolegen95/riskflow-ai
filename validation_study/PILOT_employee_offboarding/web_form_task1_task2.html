Rating rubric (click to expand — read before starting Section A)

Probability (P): 1 = Low (unlikely, no comparable occurrence in 12 months) · 2 = Medium (plausible, occurred elsewhere recently) · 3 = High (likely/recurring, has happened here before)

Vulnerability (V): 1 = Low (no known exploitable weakness) · 2 = Medium (partial/inconsistent weakness) · 3 = High (known, unaddressed weakness)

Impact (I): 1 = Low (limited, recoverable) · 2 = Medium (moderate, needs management attention) · 3 = High (severe: safety, large-scale exposure, extended outage, regulatory)

Control Effectiveness (CE): 0.00 = no control · 0.25 = manual/unverified · 0.50 = implemented, not audited · 0.75 = implemented+audited, minor gaps · 1.00 = implemented, audited, demonstrated effective
